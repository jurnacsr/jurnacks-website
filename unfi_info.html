Buying Club Letter-UNFI

Jurnack's, Naturally! is pleased to offer a unique program which allows consumers to purchase grocery, bulk foods, supplements, and personal care products near wholesale prices.  This program is offered through United Natural Foods, Inc. (UNFI), the largest natural foods distributor in the nation.  Below are the details of this exciting program:
  • Orders are due Mondays by 11 AM. You may either drop off, Fax, email or mail your order.
  • Please do not phone in your order unless it is only a few items.
  • Orders are delivered on Wednesdays. We do request orders be picked up on Wednesday but if that is not possible just let us know..
  • There is NO minimum order and payment is due when you pick up your order. However, if the pack size of what you are ordering lists more than 1 piece you are required to purchase the whole case. For example, if you are ordering yogurt and the pack size is 12/5.3oz you would be getting 12 containers of yogurt with each being 5.3 ounces. The total price listed is what you would pay.
  • There is a $20 sign-up fee and no yearly renewal fee as long as a buying club order is placed within the 12 months following sign-up.
  • Catalogs are available on-line free of charge. The procedure to view them is below.
Viewing Items and the Buying Club Catalog Online:
  1. 1. Go to www.unfi.com and click on “Customer Login” at the top of the page
  2. User Name is 'York11' and Password is 'Buyingclub1'. User name and password are case sensitive! Hit blue login bar.
  3. IMPORTANT: DO NOT MAKE ANY CHANGES TO THE WEBSITE OR PUT ANYTHING IN THE CART.
  4. Items can now be viewed using the search window. You can choose to search by any of the following: Product Description, Brand, UPC or Product Code.
  5. To view Catalogs:
    1. Click on Publications Bar
    2. Select Monthly Specials and Updates on the left side of page
      1. There will be 3 catalogs for each month.
      2. FULL Excel catalog containing all products (on special as well as regularly prices) available in our warehouse.
      3. MONTHLY Excel catalog containing only the monthly specials.
      4. Monthly PDF catalog containing only the monthly specials.
    3. In the warehouse column make sure there is a "Y" or that area is blank. This indicates the item is available in our warehouse.
    4. Choose the catalog link based on file type (EXCEL or PDF) AND “Effective From” and “Effective To” dates
    5. When you find a product you want to order just highlight the whole row and copy and paste it into an e-mail to us.

***If you have ANY questions please don’t hesitate to contact us any time.***
